The Protégé Effect?

The protégé effect is a psychological phenomenon where teaching or preparing to teach information to others helps a person learn that information.

For example, a student could benefit from the protégé effect by familiarising and improve their understanding of the material enough to be able to teach others. As they teach others, the student needs to have enough knowledge to be able to summarise the topic and answer questions posed by their peers. In such a way, both the student teacher and their peers benefit.
